好。現在研究一維反聚集：

[  
\sum_{pq\in\mathcal E}\left|\frac{h}{pq}\right|^2.  
]

這是主弧 local CLT 的中高頻核心。若它對所有非小頻 (h) 都足夠大，那麼對角 Fourier 係數就會衰減。

## 1. 最危險的情形是什麼？

若對很多候選半素數

[  
n=pq  
]

都有

[  
\left|\frac{h}{n}\right|\approx0,  
]

那就是說 (h) 接近很多 (n) 的倍數：

[  
h\approx m_n n.  
]

如果是精確整除，即

[  
n\mid h,  
]

那麼 (h) 必須含有許多不同的大素數因子。可是 (h\le L/2)，而 (L) 本身含所有素數，所以 (h) 確實可能被很多候選 (n) 整除。例如若 (h) 是某一大批素數的乘積，則任何兩個其中素數的乘積都整除 (h)。

這是個真危險！

若取

[  
h=\prod_{p\in S}p  
]

其中 (S\subset P)，則所有邊 (pq) with (p,q\in S) 都有

[  
h/(pq)\in\mathbb Z,  
]

在這些邊上完全無衰減。

這說明一維反聚集「對所有 (h)」不可能只靠半素數分佈自動成立。因為存在很多 (h) 對某些大子圖完全共振。

---

## 2. 這是不是致命？

不一定。若 (h) 是某個素數子集 (S) 的乘積，則它作為 CRT character 對應的是：

[  
\lambda_p=h\bmod p.  
]

如果 (p\in S)，則

[  
\lambda_p\equiv0\pmod p.  
]

如果 (p\notin S)，則一般非零。

這不再是「對角小頻」；它在 CRT 坐標上有一個巨大結構：很多坐標為 (0)，其他坐標為乘積 residue。

對邊 (pq) 若 (p,q\in S)，對角相位 (h/(pq)) 為整數，無衰減；但若邊跨出 (S)，即 (p\in S,\ q\notin S)，則通常

[  
h/(pq)  
]

不是整數，可能產生衰減。

所以問題轉為：候選 graph 是否有足夠多邊跨過任何非平凡子集 (S)？

這是圖 expansion 問題。

## 3. 對角大頻與 cut expansion

對角 character (h) 的「零坐標集合」：

[  
S(h)={p\in P:p\mid h}.  
]

若邊 (pq) 兩端都在 (S(h))，則 (pq\mid h)，相位為整數，無衰減。

若兩端都不在 (S(h))，也可能不整除，但沒有簡單分類。

至少，所有 crossing edges

[  
p\in S(h),\quad q\notin S(h)  
]

中，對 (p\mid h) 而 (q\nmid h)，有

[  
\frac{h}{pq}=\frac{h/p}{q}.  
]

其小數部分取決於

[  
(h/p)\bmod q.  
]

若 (q\nmid h)，這是非零 residue；它可以離整數很近嗎？作為

[  
\frac{a}{q}  
]

的形式，距離至少

[  
1/q.  
]

這可能很小，但若跨邊很多，平方和為

[  
\sum_{\text{cross}}\frac{1}{q^2}  
]

可能仍足夠？

若 (q\sim2^k)，每項最低只有 (2^{-2k})。跨邊數若約 (2^{2k}/k)，則平方和約 (1/k)，不大但可累積。需要對求和要求精算。

---

## 4. 這揭示一個重要點

主弧「大對角頻率」其實不是純一維問題，它與 CRT 坐標的 support 結構有關。若 (h) 被很多頂點素數整除，則對角 character 在那些坐標為 (0)，相當於離真正的小對角 (h=0) 很近於某些坐標、遠於另一些坐標。

因此大對角頻率可以由**非對角/cut analysis**處理，而不完全依靠一維 local CLT。

這是好消息。它說：

- 小 (h)：真正主弧，用 Gaussian；
    
- 大 (h) 且沒有很多 prime divisibility：一維反聚集；
    
- 大 (h) 且有很多 prime divisibility：graph cut edges 給衰減。
    

這比先前「所有對角高頻靠一維」更細。

## 5. 對 graph 的要求

我們需要候選 graph (\mathcal E) 對任意非平凡頂點子集 (S) 有大量 crossing edges：

[  
E(S,S^c).  
]

Dyadic block graph 很稠密，應有良好 expansion，至少在尺度圖連通範圍內。

但如果 (S) 是整個前綴尺度集合，例如所有小尺度 primes，crossing edges 只在尺度邊界附近。因我們只連接 (2/3\le k/\ell\le3/2)，尺度圖本身是局部連接而非 complete，前綴 cut 的 crossing mass 可能較小。

這可能成為問題。

解法：允許尺度圖有長程邊？但 Irving 只在 (x\in[Q^{2/3},Q^{3/2}]) 範圍內。尺度圖在 log-scale 上仍有 multiplicative expansion：從 (k) 可到 (1.5k)。前綴 cut ([k_0,T]) 到外部有 edges from levels near (T) to up to (1.5T)，其 crossing mass約

[  
\sum_{k\sim T}\frac1k  
]

可能為常數或 (1)，不是太小。應該夠。

## 6. 更自然的主弧分類

不要只按整數 (h) 大小分類，而按

[  
S(h)={p:p\mid h}  
]

分類。

- 若 (S(h)=\varnothing) 或很小，則 (h/(pq)) 通常不接近整數，靠一維反聚集。
    
- 若 (S(h)) 很大但不是全部，則 crossing edges (S)-(S^c) 衰減。
    
- 若 (S(h)=P)，則 (L\mid h)，但 (0<|h|<L) 不可能。若 (h=0)，這是唯一零頻主項。
    

小非零 (h) 沒有任何 (p\mid h) because all (p) large, so (S(h)=\varnothing)，但小 (h/(pq)) 都很小，這是 Gaussian主弧而非衰減。所以還要按大小。

因此：

1. (|h|\le H_0\sim1/\sigma)：主弧。
    
2. (|h|>H_0) 且 (h) not divisible by many graph vertices：一維/multiscale反聚集。
    
3. (|h|>H_0) 且 divisible by many vertices：cut expansion.

## 7. 小心：若 (h) 是所有候選頂點除少數外的乘積

例如

[  
h=L/r  
]

則 (h\le L/2) 且被除了 (r) 外所有素數整除。對所有邊不接 (r)，相位整數；只有接 (r) 的邊衰減。若 (r) 的 degree 很大，仍可殺掉。這要求每個頂點 degree 足夠大。Dyadic graph中 degree 巨大，所以可行。

此類 (h=L/r) 是最危險的高頻：只靠一個頂點的 incident edges 衰減。degree約巨大，應足以壓過這類 (h) 的數量（約頂點數）。

更一般 (h=L/M) where (M) product of small set (T); 衰減來自 boundary of (T) or incident edges to (T). 若 (T) small, degree times |T| huge.

這和 graph cut expansion 完全一致。

## 8. 可提出一個 graph aperiodicity lemma

令 (G=(P,\mathcal E)) 是候選 graph。對每個非零 (h\bmod L)，定義

[  
T(h)={p\in P:p\nmid h}.  
]

邊 (pq) 若兩端都不在 (T(h))，即 (p,q\mid h)，則無衰減。其他邊可能衰減。

如果 (T(h)) 很小，衰減主要來自 incident edges of (T(h))。

如果 (T(h)) 很大，很多邊至少一端不除 (h)，應有大量相位非整數。

需要證：

[  
\sum_{pq\in\mathcal E}  
\left|\frac{h}{pq}\right|^2  
\gg \Psi(h),  
]

其中 (\Psi(h)) 至少與 boundary/degree of (T(h)) 成正比，足以求和。

最粗下界：若 (q\nmid h) 而 (p\mid h)，則

# [  
\left|\frac{h}{pq}\right|

\left|\frac{h/p}{q}\right|  
\ge \frac1q.  
]

平方為 (1/q^2)。如果 incident edges to (q) 很多 with (p\mid h)，總和約 degree weighted by (1/q^2)。對 (q\sim2^k)，若 degree到相似尺度約 (2^k/k) 或更多，得到約

[  
\frac{2^k/k}{2^{2k}}=\frac1{k2^k},  
]

很小。這可能不夠！ 之前我按固定相位距離估算，但對 (h=L/q) crossing phase 最小只有 (1/q)。

這是嚴重問題。

## 11. High diagonal frequencies split by divisibility set works

This suggests a robust approach:

For diagonal h, define support

[  
T={p: h\not\equiv0\pmod p}.  
]

On edges between (T) and (P\setminus T), phase reduces to single inverse term from the (T)-vertex. Irving gives constant-distance for many such edges.

On edges inside (T), both terms nonzero; may still decay but not needed.

On edges outside (T), phase zero.

So if the cut (\partial T) is large, we get decay.

If (T=P), i.e. h divisible by none of the primes, then no cut. This includes small h. For small h,主弧. For large h with T=P, need one-dimensional反聚集 or maybe original phase h/(pq) with no divisibility. If (h) not divisible by any p and large, (h/(pq)) should oscillate; need handle separately.

Thus two cases:

- (T) nontrivial: cut + Irving.
    
- (T=P): h coprime to L; pure one-dimensional oscillation.
    

The latter may be handled by choosing scale pq around h as before. Since h not divisible by p,q, exact near divisibility by pq is rare.

This is manageable.

## 12. Current bottleneck narrowed further

Main arc high-frequency problem reduces to:

1. **Divisibility support case**: handled by graph expansion + Irving on cut edges.
    
2. **Coprime high h**: need show many (pq) have (h/(pq)) away from integers.
    

The coprime case is a genuine one-dimensional anti-concentration but easier because no exact divisibility by candidate primes.

For coprime (h), for fixed q, phase as p varies has both terms but equivalent to (h/(pq)). The condition (|h/(pq)|<\eta) means

[  
h \equiv r \pmod{pq}  
]

with small residue relative to (pq), or h near multiple. Since pq ranges over many semiprimes and none divides h, should not happen for most.

This may be attacked by divisor bounds / distribution of fractional parts of h/n over semiprimes.

## 13. Practical route

For proof, perhaps avoid deep one-dimensional divisor analysis by adding a small set of very low-scale smoothing primes? If include candidate edges with (pq) covering all scales up to h, for every h there is a scale with (pq\approx h), giving constant phase h/pq for all those edges. For h larger than max pq, choose scale near a divisor of h? Not always.

But since max pq in graph can be enormous, for h≤L/2 always there is a scale (pq\approx h) if h within range of edge weights. If h exceeds largest pq, then h/(pq) large. Fractional parts could be arbitrary, but varying pq in a scale near (\sqrt h)? We can include scales up to L? impossible, L enormous. Edges max pq is about (2^{2K}), while h can be up to L/2, vastly larger.

So high h beyond all pq must be handled by modular/divisor methods, not scale matching.

This is exactly lattice local CLT high-frequency issue.

## 14. Maybe high h with T=P impossible for very high h?

T=P means h is coprime to every prime in P, i.e. ((h,L)=1). There are many such h up to L.

For such h, map (n=pq) has h mod n nonzero. Need anti-concentration of h/n. This resembles showing set of weights (w_n=L/n) has no large common period: for frequency h, phases (h w_n/L=h/n). If h coprime to L, multiplication by h permutes (\mathbb Z/L), but weights are special.

Could use additive combinatorics: since gcd of weights is 1, high frequency cannot align with all weights unless h small? But gcd=1 alone gives no quantitative bound.

Need stronger aperiodicity.

## 15. This is likely where existing local CLT tools matter

Weighted Bernoulli local CLT theorems often assume a condition like

[  
\sup_{t\in[\delta,\pi]} \prod_i |\phi_i(t)|\to0  
]

or equivalent maximal span condition. They don't prove it automatically from max weight small. For arbitrary weights, local CLT can fail due to periodicity. We need verify for (1/(pq)) weights.

This is a real remaining hard point.

Maybe Littlewood-Offord inverse theorems help: if concentration high, weights lie mostly in a generalized arithmetic progression of small rank. Our integer weights (L/(pq)) might not, because of multiplicative structure. But proving that could be nontrivial.

## 16. Updated honest assessment

We have:

- 次弧非對角: likely strong via Irving.
    
- 對角 high-frequency with divisibility support: likely via Irving + expansion.
    
- 對角 high-frequency coprime to L: still hard; requires aperiodicity/local CLT for semiprime reciprocal weights.
    

This is now the sharpest bottleneck.

So next step should be to formulate this as a standalone lemma:

**Semiprime reciprocal aperiodicity lemma.**  
For dyadic block graph semiprimes (n=pq), and (L=\prod_{p\in P}p), for every (h) with ((h,L)=1) and (1/\sigma\ll h\le L/2),

[  
\sum_{n\in\mathcal N}\left|\frac{h}{n}\right|^2  
]

is large enough.

If this lemma can be proved or found in local CLT literature, the main route may close. If not, this is the real new problem.

Thus next, we should try to attack this lemma directly or search specifically for local CLT/aperiodicity criteria that can verify it.
