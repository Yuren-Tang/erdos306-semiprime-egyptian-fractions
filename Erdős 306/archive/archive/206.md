## 1. 正確主定理範圍

若所有分母都是 squarefree semiprimes，則所有分母的 lcm 是 squarefree，故有限和的既約分母必 squarefree。

## 6. Naive Helly / agreement route is false

Bounded-window CRT Helly is false.

Example:

\[
L_5=\{n\in[-11,11]: n\equiv0\pmod5\}
=\{-10,-5,0,5,10\},

\[
L_7=\{n\in[-11,11]: n\equiv3\pmod7\}
=\{-11,-4,3,10\},

\[
L_{11}=\{n\in[-11,11]: n\equiv0\pmod{11}\}
=\{-11,0,11\}.
\]

Pairwise intersections:

\[
L_5\cap L_7=\{10\},\quad
L_5\cap L_{11}=\{0\},\quad
L_7\cap L_{11}=\{-11\}.
\]

But

\[
L_5\cap L_7\cap L_{11}=\varnothing.
\]

Therefore:

\[
\text{pairwise bounded CRT intersections}
\not\Rightarrow
\text{common bounded representative}.
\]

This invalidates the naive good-graph agreement proof.

## 7. What remains plausible

The false Helly example does **not** disprove single-block CRT Poincaré. It only says the proof cannot be zero-one good-graph agreement.

3. the correct mechanism is quadratic/spectral, not Helly.

Thus the remaining local theorem should be understood as a weighted CRT-code local testability / Hodge-Poincaré statement.

## 8. Bounded cocycle rigidity lemma

This is a genuine partial result.

Suppose edge labels \(H_{pq}\) satisfy CRT triangle congruences, and on a subset \(S\subset P\),

\[
|H_{pq}|\le KX
\quad(p,q\in S).
\]

Choose base edge \(p_0q_0\), set

\[
m=H_{p_0q_0}.
\]

For \(r\in S\),

\[
H_{p_0r}=m+\alpha_r p_0,\qquad
H_{q_0r}=m+\beta_r q_0,
\]

with

\[
|\alpha_r|,|\beta_r|\ll K.
\]

Since both are congruent to \(a_r\pmod r\),

\[
r\mid \alpha_rp_0-\beta_rq_0.
\]

If \((\alpha_r,\beta_r)=(0,0)\), then \(r\) obeys label \(m\). Otherwise \(r\) lies in a bad divisor set.

For fixed base edge, crude bad count:

\[
O(K^2).
\]

By averaging over base edges, plausible/partly derivable improvement:

\[
O(K^2/\log X)
\]

in the range \(K^2\ll X\).

This gives low-energy single-block compression up to roughly

\[
R\ll X/\log^2X

or at least

R\ll X/\log^3X
